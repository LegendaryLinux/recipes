# Vanilla Cake

## Cake

### Ingredients
- 2.33 cups all-purpose flour
- 1.5 cups white sugar
- 2.25 tsp baking powder
- 0.25 tsp salt
- 3 large eggs
- 0.75 cup whole milk
- 0.75 cup vegetable oil
- 1 tbs vanilla extract

### Directions
1. Preheat oven to 350F
2. Mix flour, sugar, baking powder, and salt together in a large bowl
3. Add eggs, milk, vegetable oil, and vanilla
4. Mix until batter is smooth
5. Bake 45 - 50 minutes. Use a toothpick to ensure center is dry
6. Let cool for five minutes
7. Invert cake onto cooling rack
8. Let cake cool completely


# Icing

## Ingredients
- 5 cups powdered sugar
- 1 cup unsalted butter, softened
- 2 tsp vanilla extract
- 2 - 3 tbs whole milk
- 0.25 tsp salt

### Directions
1. Mix powdered subar and butter until combined
2. Mix vigorously for about one minute, until light and fluffy
3. Add vanilla, 2 tbs milk, and salt
4. Mix until fully incorporated
5. Slowly add milk until frosting is smooth and easy to spread, about 0.5 tsp at a time
6. Add any additional ingredients to frosting
7. Mix one additional minute
8. Use immediately

### Notes:
- If frosting is too thick, add 0.25tsp of milk
- If frosting is too thin, add 1 tbs powdered sugar
