# Chocolate Chip Cookies
__Ingredients:__
- 1 cup unsalted butter, softened
- 1.25 cups light brown sugar
- 0.75 cup granulated sugar
- 2 tsp vanilla extract
- 2 eggs + 1 yolk
- 2.75 cups all-purpose flour
- 1 tsp baking soda
- 0.25 tsp baking powder
- 1 tsp sea salt
- 1.5 cups chocolate chips

__Directions:__
1. Allow butter to soften outside of refrigerator
2. Mix butter and sugars in a bowl until light and airy (cream)
3. Add eggs and yolk to the mix, one at a time, still mixing
4. Add vanilla to bowl, keep mixing
5. Mix all dry ingredients in a separate bowl (flour, baking soda, baking powder, sea salt)
6. Slowly add mixed dry ingredients to bowl, and mix just until combined
7. Add chocolate chips until it feels right
8. Cover bowl and chill in refrigerator for 60 minutes
9. Set oven to 350 F
10. Place cookie dough on a baking tray with parchment paper
11. Bake for 14 to 15 minutes, remove when edges are set, and centers look slightly under-done
12. Let cookies sit for fifteen minutes
13. Sprinkle a bit of sea salt on top of the cookies
